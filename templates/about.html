About Our Project

We are Grade 12 students from Siena College of Taytay, Taytay Rizal in the Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics Strand. As part of our Inquiries, Investigation and Immersion/Capstone Project, we are conducting a groundbreaking study titled:

"Visual Pill Identification Website: Enhancing Medication Adherence for Cognitive Impairment"

Our Mission

To develop a Visual Pill Identification Platform that improves medication adherence among patients with cognitive impairments, ensuring correct medication administration and enhancing patient safety.

Project Impact

Our platform assists patients, nurses, and healthcare workers by:

  • Ensuring accurate medication identification
  • Improving patient safety
  • Reducing healthcare burdens
  • Supporting cognitive impairment patients
